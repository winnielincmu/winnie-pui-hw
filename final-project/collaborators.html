Seraphine

Featured Artist

Seraphine is a a vibrant young producer and songwriter who recently collaborated with K/DA. Determined to reach new heights, Seraphine uses music as a way to connect with others and herself. Her earnest and open-hearted energy transcends across cultures as she hopes to unite the world through her craft. Seraphine is voiced by Lexie Liu and Jasmine Clarke.

After gaining attention for her talents on social media, the girls of K/DA invited her to join in on their track "More." On her own time, Seraphine has also released songs, such as "Made Me This Way," for fans to listen to. K/DA looks forward to her success as an up and coming artist in the industry.
